Modern Lifestyle Theme - Icon Set

The function of an icon is to simplify the image, it is usually a symbol of something. Icons are important nowadays because people can easily recognize them not only on technology devices but also in everything we see in our daily life.

The theme of the icon set that I created is based on the modern lifestyle. There are 10 icons in this life set: calendar, camera, clock, email, messenger, music, note, recording, store and weather. Although these icons are simple plus people see them almost every day, the most important idea is the design concept. Every icon has its colour to represent itself. Because of their colourful feature, it usually catches people’s eyes. Furthermore, the edges of each icon are designed to be rounded because it is more comfortable for people when they are looking at them.

Calender

The calendar is to identify days, add plans or make notes on the specific date. The holidays will also be marked on the calendar.

Camera

The camera can capture and record stunning photos and videos include landscape, portrait, animals, night view…etc. by using high-quality resolution.

Clock

A clock is a useful tool that can help people check the time anywhere, with and without the internet. People can also set an alarm or a timer for their needs.

Email

Email is the most common tool for communication these days, mails are mostly replaced by them. More people can send and receive emails within a short period of time.

Messenge

Messenge is a convenient tool when people want to build social contacts, make connections with people especially during self-isolated period.

Music

Music is built-in software that provides 99% of the music within the world. People can download music on the software and listen to them when offline.

Note

The note is like a word document, people can type anything on it. For example, self-reminder, shopping lists, ideas...etc.

Recorder

The recorder could record a voice memo, music pieces, and an important conversation.

Store

The store is a built-in app that provides launched applications for technology devices, people can find almost everything they need on the app store.

Weather

Weather provides accurate information about temperate and humidity, it also predicts the weather for the week. The information changes with the updated weather.
